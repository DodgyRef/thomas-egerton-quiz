Round FOUR – WHO AM I? 
I WAS BORN IN WALES IN 1968.  MY MIDDLE NAME IS DANIEL.  MY PARENTS LIVED IN ENGLAND AT THE TIME OF MY BIRTH, BUT MY FATHER DROVE OVER THE BORDER WHEN MY MOTHER WENT INTO LABOUR TO ENSURE I WAS BORN IN WALES.
I GREW UP IN THE ENGLISH TOWN OF WEM, WHICH I REGARD AS MY HOME.  I WAS EDUCATED AT THOMAS ADAMS SCHOOL IN WEM, BEFORE STUDYING DRAMA AND ENGLISH BRUNEL UNIVERSITY IN UXBRIDGE, FROM WHERE I GRADUATED IN 1990.
BEFORE ENTERING COMEDY FULL-TIME IN MY 30s, I SPENT THIRTEEN YEARS WORKING AS A DRAMA TEACHER. I OFTEN JOKE THAT MY CAREER CHANGE WAS FUELLED BY A LACK OF AUTHORITY AND MY GENUINE FEAR OF MY OWN STUDENTS.
MY MAINSTREAM ACTING BREAKTHROUGH CAME WHEN I WAS CAST AS THE STUDENT-HATING HEAD OF SIXTH FORM, MR GILBERT, IN THE HIT E4 SITCOM THE INBETWEENERS. I ALSO WROTE AND STARRED IN THE SITCOM MAN DOWN, AS WELL AS PLAYING A LAWYER IN THE SITCOM CUCKOO STARRING LONGSIDE ADAM SAMBERG & TAYLOR LAUTNER.
I AM MOST FAMOUS FOR MY ROLE AS THE "ALL-POWERFUL" JUDGE ON LONG-RUNNING DAVE/CHANNEL 4 SERIES TASKMASTER. SITTING ON MY THRONE ALONGSIDE MY ASSISTANT ALEX HORNE, I JUDGE CELEBRITIES AS THEY PERFORM BIZARRE TASKS TO WIN A TROPHY OF MY OWN GOLDEN HEAD.
